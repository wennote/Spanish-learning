Read Me

This website is a personal Spanish study dashboard based on your first five days of notes. It is designed to keep the early learning material in one place so you can review verbs, vocabulary, sentence patterns, and question forms without searching across separate files. The Vocabulary tab groups useful words and model phrases from the lessons. The Daily Notes tab keeps each day's learning points organized, including regular verbs, ser and estar, key verbs such as ir, tener, hacer, and querer, basic question patterns, family vocabulary, and simple past tense. The Practice tab now focuses on two things: checking mistakes carefully and writing short Spanish responses from English prompts. The Feedback tab lets you write comments locally in the browser so you can keep track of what is clear, what is confusing, and what should be improved in later versions.

Current Sections

  • Vocabulary: words, verbs, and short example phrases from Days 1 to 5.
  • Daily Notes: lesson-by-lesson summaries from your study files.
  • Stories: short reading passages and cartoon scenes using learned vocabulary.
  • Practice: quiz review with explanations and short English-to-Spanish writing prompts.
  • Feedback: local notes about problems, ideas, and learning progress.
