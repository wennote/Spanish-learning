Read Me

This website is a personal Spanish study dashboard based on your first four days of notes. It keeps the early learning material in one place so you can review verbs, vocabulary, sentence patterns, question forms, and short practice tasks without searching across separate files. The Vocabulary tab now includes topic filters, pronunciation buttons, and a way to mark difficult words for spaced review. The Daily Notes tab keeps each day’s key ideas organized. The Practice tab now includes flashcards, a daily mini quiz with score tracking, explanations for wrong answers, and a difficult-word review mode. The Feedback tab stores your comments locally in the browser. The Translate tab helps you turn a short English paragraph into basic Spanish using patterns from your current lessons.

Current Sections

  • Vocabulary: words, verbs, question forms, topic filters, and pronunciation audio.
  • Daily Notes: lesson-by-lesson summaries from Days 1 to 4.
  • Practice: flashcards, daily mini quiz, score history, and difficult-word review.
  • Feedback: local notes about progress, errors, and ideas.
  • Translate: simple English to Spanish paragraph help based on your current study level.

Important Note

  • Pronunciation audio uses your browser’s built-in speech voice, so voice quality depends on the Mac and browser.
  • Translation is intentionally simple and matches your current beginner content. It is not a full translator.
  • When a quiz answer is wrong, the site now explains why the correct choice fits the rule.
